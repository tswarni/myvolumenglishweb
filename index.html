About MyVolumeEnglish

MyVolumeEnglish is an innovative platform designed to help you improve your English through real CNN news articles. Our system categorizes articles by English proficiency level (CEFR), making it easy for learners at any stage to find content that challenges but doesn't overwhelm them.

Features:

  • 📰 Real CNN news articles
  • 🎯 Content matched to your level
  • 📚 CEFR level classification (A1-C2)
  • 🎨 Beautiful, easy-to-read interface
  • 💾 Track your reading progress
  • 📝 Word definitions and explanations

How It Works:

  1. Select your English level (Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced)
  2. Browse curated CNN news articles
  3. Read articles with vocabulary support
  4. Learn useful vocabulary and expressions
  5. Progress to higher levels as you improve
